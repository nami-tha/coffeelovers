CAPPUCCINO

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Austria with later development taking place in Italy, and is prepared with steamed milk foam.Variations of the drink involve the use of cream instead of milk

ICEDCOFFEE

Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee in the normal way and then serving it over ice or in cold milk or by brewing the coffee cold.Pre-packaged iced coffee is available as a grocery item in several countries, though without ice

MOCHA

A café mocha, also called mocaccino, is a chocolate-flavoured warm beverage that is a variant of a café latte, commonly served in a glass rather than a mug. Other commonly used spellings are mochaccino and also mochachino.
